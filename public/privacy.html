Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026 · Operated by Nicholas Barcenas

Aveum is a personal energy optimisation tool. This policy explains what data we collect, why we collect it, and how we use it. We keep this simple because we believe you deserve to understand it.

What we collect

  • Energy account credentials — API keys and account numbers for services like Octopus Energy, GivEnergy, Zappi, and other connected devices. These are used solely to read your energy data and are never shared with third parties.
  • Energy usage data — electricity prices, solar generation, battery state, EV charging sessions. This is used to optimise your system and show you your savings.
  • Device information — the types of devices you connect (solar inverter, battery, EV charger, smart meter).

What we do not collect

  • We do not collect your name, address, or payment details.
  • We do not sell your data to anyone, ever.
  • We do not use your data for advertising.
  • We do not share your data with third parties except the APIs you explicitly connect to (e.g. Octopus Energy) in order to retrieve your own data.

How we use your data

Your data is used only to power the Aveum dashboard — showing you live energy prices, optimising when your battery charges and exports, scheduling your EV charging, and calculating your savings. We do not use it for any other purpose.

Data storage

Credentials and energy data are stored securely. We use industry-standard encryption in transit and at rest. We do not store more data than is necessary to run the service.

Your rights

You can delete your account and all associated data at any time by contacting us. You can also disconnect any device at any time from within the app, which will stop us accessing that device's data immediately.

Third-party services

Aveum connects to third-party APIs (Octopus Energy, GivEnergy, myenergi, etc.) on your behalf. When you provide credentials for these services, you are subject to their privacy policies in addition to this one. We only read data from these services — we never write or make changes without your explicit instruction.
