Clear existing schedules before Aveum takes over

If your EV charger or battery inverter already has a charging schedule set in its own app, Aveum and the device will both try to control charging at the same time. This causes conflicts that reduce savings. Take two minutes to clear any existing schedules first.

Why this matters: Two conflicting schedules can cause your battery to charge at peak prices or your EV to ignore Aveum's cheaper window. Clearing existing schedules is the single most impactful step before going live.
Ohme ✓ Supported
  1. Open the Ohme app and tap Schedule.
  2. Tap the schedule you want to remove and select Delete.
  3. Repeat for any secondary or recurring schedules listed.
  4. Confirm the schedule list is empty before returning to Aveum.
myenergi Zappi ✓ Supported
  1. Open the myenergi app and tap your Zappi device.
  2. Select Schedule or Boost schedule.
  3. Tap each schedule entry and choose Remove.
  4. Set Zappi to Eco+ mode — Aveum will manage charge timing from here.
Easee ✓ Supported
  1. Open the Easee app and select your charger.
  2. Tap Smart Charging and disable any active schedules or Smart Charging profiles.
  3. Set the charger to Manual mode — Aveum will take over from here.
Hypervolt ✓ Supported
  1. Open the Hypervolt app and go to Schedule.
  2. Disable or delete all existing scheduled charge windows.
  3. Leave the charger in Ready state — Aveum controls timing.
Wallbox ✓ Supported
  1. Open the myWallbox app and tap your charger.
  2. Go to Schedule and delete any existing charge programmes.
  3. Ensure Smart Charging is turned off in the charger settings — Aveum manages this.
Pod Point ✓ Supported
  1. Open the Pod Point app and navigate to Schedules.
  2. Delete all existing schedules.
  3. Set your charger to Anytime mode so Aveum can override it freely.
Indra ✓ Supported
  1. Open the Indra app and select Smart Schedules.
  2. Delete all existing schedules.
  3. Confirm the charger shows Ready before proceeding.
GivEnergy Battery ✓ Supported
  1. Log into givenergy.cloud.
  2. Go to My Devices → your inverter → Inverter Settings.
  3. Under Schedule, clear or disable any Charge / Discharge / Export schedules.
  4. Set the inverter to Eco mode — Aveum will programme charge windows via the API.
Tesla (via OAuth) ✓ Supported
  1. In the Tesla app, tap your car → Charging.
  2. Select Scheduled Charging and turn it Off.
  3. Select Scheduled Departure and turn it Off.
  4. Aveum will now manage your car's charge timing directly.

Once you've cleared existing schedules, return to Aveum onboarding to complete setup. If you have questions, contact us at hello@aveum.co.uk.
